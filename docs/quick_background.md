# Star Furniture Goa Website

## Company

Star Furniture Goa designs and manufactures custom furniture specializing in engineered wood and plywood.

The website should feel premium, modern, elegant and minimal.

Target audience:
- Homeowners
- Architects
- Interior Designers
- Commercial Clients

Primary Goal:
Generate enquiries through WhatsApp and showroom visits.
